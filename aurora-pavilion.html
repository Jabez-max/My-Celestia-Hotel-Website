Frequently Asked Questions about Aurora Pavilion

What are Aurora Pavilion's operating hours? ⌄

We are open daily to serve you. Breakfast is available from 6:00 AM to 10:30 AM. Lunch is served from 12:00 NN to 2:30 PM, and our grand Dinner Buffet runs from 6:00 PM to 10:30 PM.

Do you cater to dietary restrictions or allergies? ⌄

Yes, absolutely. Our culinary team is more than happy to accommodate special dietary requirements including gluten-free, vegan, and halal options. Please inform our staff of any allergies upon making a reservation or before ordering.

Does Aurora Pavilion have a kids' menu? ⌄

Yes, we offer a specially curated kids' menu featuring nutritious and kid-friendly favorites. For our buffets, children aged 5 and below dine for free, while those aged 6 to 12 enjoy a 50% discount.

Is parking available for restaurant guests? ⌄

Yes, dining guests are entitled to complimentary valet parking and self-parking at the Celestia Hotel basement level. Please have your parking ticket validated at the restaurant reception after your meal.
