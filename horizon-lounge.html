Horizon Lounge

Your 24-Hour Sanctuary for Signature Sips and Bites

Step into the Horizon Lounge, Celestia Hotel's vibrant social heartbeat. Where culinary artistry meets a sophisticated ambiance, savor expertly crafted tapas, indulge in delightful gourmet bites, and sip on signature cocktails that tantalize your taste buds.

As the sun sets over the spectacular Bonifacio Global City skyline, the Lounge transforms into an enchanting evening venue with live acoustic entertainment. The sophisticated yet relaxed atmosphere makes it the ultimate destination for modern travelers and locals alike.

Whether it’s a casual afternoon catch-up over premium coffee, a quick business meeting, or a sophisticated night out with friends, the Horizon Lounge promises unforgettable moments and impeccable service in every visit.

Frequently Asked Questions about Horizon Lounge

What are Horizon Lounge operating hours? ⌄

We are proudly open 24 hours daily. Whether you need a morning espresso, a mid-day snack, or late-night cocktails, our doors are always open to serve you.

Do you cater to dietary restrictions or allergies? ⌄

Absolutely! We offer various options including vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free light bites. Please inform our staff of any specific dietary needs or allergies when placing your order.

Do you offer delivery or takeaway options? ⌄

Our food and handcrafted beverages are designed for dine-in to ensure the best quality and presentation. However, select pastries, sandwiches, and bottled beverages are available for takeaway upon request.

Is parking available? ⌄

Yes, complimentary valet and self-parking are available for all guests visiting the Horizon Lounge at Celestia Hotel.
