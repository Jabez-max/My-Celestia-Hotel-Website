Discover our 5-star hotel in the heart of the metropolis

Located at the vibrant center of the city skyline, Celestia Hotel stands proud as a beacon of luxury. This modern 15-story, 5-star hotel is convenient for those seeking an elevated lifestyle, as well as for those looking to explore the main historic sights, as the neighborhood is located between the central business district and the bay area.

This part of the city is directly adjacent to premium shopping and business districts, entertainment parks, and conference centers. Celestia Hotel opened its doors to guests to provide 450 well-appointed rooms. Outside, its architecture is modern, but once you enter its airy lobby, it transforms into a haven of classic elegance.
